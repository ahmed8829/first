our mission

A better planet, one yard at a time

It started with two brothers and one radical idea: Home and lawn care
should be better. Better for you, better for your family, and better
for the planet.

CUTURE

Help us change lawn care
by doing what you love

At Sunday, we work hard to cultivate an
engaging, open atmosphere where our
diverse team can connect,share ideas, and
reimagine the American backyard. Our
culture reflects our commitment to
inclusion, individual growth, and curiosity.
Together we are building a brand that
empowers everyone to grow better.
The only thing missing is you!
